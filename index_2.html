Research & Interviews

RESEARCH were conducted to define holistic wellbeing and explore current existing applications and tools.
INTERVIEWS were conducted focused on people`s history with injuries, stories about self-motivations and experiences with Wellness applications.


Wellness Definition

Wellness refers to diverse and interconnected dimensions of physical, mental, and social well-being that extend beyond the traditional definition of health. It includes choices and activities aimed at achieving physical vitality, mental alacrity, social satisfaction, a sense of accomplishment, and personal fulfillment.

Categories of Wellness

Physical Health

  • Physical Activity
  • Nutrition & Diet
  • Bad habits and their control
  • Medical Care
  • Rest & Sleep

Mental Health

  • Cognitive well-being
  • Behavioral well-being
  • Emotional well-being

Social Health

  • Communication and Interaction
  • Meaningful relationships
  • Maintaining a good support network
