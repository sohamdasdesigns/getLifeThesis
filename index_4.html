AI Exploration

How pose estimation works?

Phase 1

  1. AI is fed with numerous number of exercise & body pose data (Videos, Images etc) for it to learn

  2. AI process & learns to understand and differentiate each body pose or workout movement

  3. Makes a Data model for each pose and workout movement and Stores it for future use


Phase 2

  1. When someone is doing workout with AI, it makes a new model with body points

  2. Then AI compares the new model with the previous one from Phase 1

  3. If both matches, then AI check the pose as correct, else gives instruction for correction
