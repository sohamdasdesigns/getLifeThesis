Let`s put it together

How to use the exploration till now in getLife ecosystem?

1

  • Users Align the details of their injuries/problems in getLife application
  • Based on their area of choices they are sceheduled with Physiotherapist Online session
  • They are provided with Custom Prescription on basis of their personal injuries/medical history


2

  • Physiotherapist Session
  • With physiotherapist`s instructions, trainee tries the exercises already provided in Technogym application
  • Their own best repetitions are recorded and uploaded to getLife AI for processing


3

  • Personal Training Session
  • While doing workout with getLife AI checks user`s posture and compares with her own previous posture (Data) model
  • When she achieves her best, AI Coach guides & motivates her to achieve the Technogym professional Coach`s (Data) model


4

  • Physiotherapist / Coach talk sessions
  • In the physiotherapist / Coach talk session users will be provided with advices to improve their workouts or solutions to the issues they are having
  • To provide more human interaction to their personal session with AI Coach
